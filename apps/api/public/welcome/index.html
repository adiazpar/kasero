Frequently asked questions

Is Kasero really free?

Yes. Sales recording, inventory, barcode scanning, team roles, and data export are free. If we ever add paid tiers, nothing that is free today gets taken away — that is our public promise.

Can I manage more than one business?

Yes — that is what Kasero is built for. Create as many businesses as you run under one account and switch between them instantly, each with its own products, sales, team, and currency.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Kasero is offline-first: keep selling with no signal at the market or on the road, and everything syncs when you are back online.

Does Kasero process payments?

No. Kasero records sales in whatever payment method you accept, but your money never passes through us. There are no processing fees and no risk of frozen funds.

What languages does Kasero support?

Eleven: English, Spanish, Portuguese, Filipino, Vietnamese, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese. Each business also sets its own currency and number formats.

Can I export my data?

Always. Your sales history, expenses, and inventory can be exported at any time, and this will never move behind a paywall.
