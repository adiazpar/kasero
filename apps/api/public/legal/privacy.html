Kasero Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Kasero is a point-of-sale and inventory app for small businesses. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, where it is stored, and what control you have over it. We have tried to write it plainly, because we actually want you to read it.

The short version: we store the account and business data you give us so the app works; we send product and receipt photos to an AI provider only when you use the AI features; we show no ads, we never sell your data, and we never process your payments. You can export your data at any time and delete your account from inside the app.

1. Data we collect

Account data. When you create an account we store your name, your email address, and your language preference. You can sign in with a one-time email code, or with Google or Apple. If you use Sign in with Apple and choose to hide your email, we receive and store the private-relay address Apple generates (an address ending in @privaterelay.appleid.com) instead of your real one — we never see your real email in that case.

Business data. Everything you enter to run your businesses: business names and settings, products, categories, prices, stock levels, sales records, expenses, sessions, team members and their roles, and notes. This data exists so the app can do its job — it is your business record, not a marketing asset.

Photos. Product photos and receipt photos you take or upload, including logos and product images you attach.

Technical data. Standard operational data needed to keep the service secure and working: session tokens, timestamps, and server logs (which include IP addresses for abuse prevention and rate limiting). We do not run third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

2. Where your data is stored

Account and business data is stored in a managed database operated by Turso (a libSQL/SQLite cloud database service), and our application is hosted on cloud infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit. Turso acts as a data processor on our behalf and does not use your data for its own purposes.

3. AI features and third-party processing

When you use Kasero's AI features — for example, snapping a photo to add a product — the photo you take is sent to fal.ai, a third-party AI processing service, which analyzes the image and returns structured product details (name, category, description). This happens only when you actively use an AI feature; photos are never sent for AI processing in the background or without your action. fal.ai processes these images on our behalf to provide the feature.

4. What we do NOT do

  • No ads. Kasero contains no advertising and no ad-tech SDKs.
  • No sale of data. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data or your business data to anyone.
  • No payment processing. Kasero records your sales but never touches your money. We do not collect, transmit, or store card numbers or payment credentials, because payments never flow through us.

5. Your data is exportable — always

You can export your sales history and business data from the app at any time. This is a public product commitment: your sales history and exports are never taken away, and export will never be moved behind a paywall.

6. Deleting your account

You can delete your account directly in the app (Account page, then Delete Account). Deletion removes your account data and the business data of businesses you own, as described on our account deletion page. If you signed in with Apple, we also revoke the Sign in with Apple token as required by Apple. If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by email (see Contact below) and we will verify the request and process it.

7. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, personal data and owned-business data are deleted from the production database; residual copies in encrypted backups and server logs expire on a rolling basis within a limited retention window. Data in businesses owned by other people (for example, sales you recorded as a team member of someone else's business) remains part of that business's records, attributed without your deleted personal details.

8. Team members and shared businesses

If you join a business as a team member, the business owner and other authorized members can see the activity you record there (such as sales you enter and your name and role). The business owner controls that business's data.

9. Children

Kasero is a business tool and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the higher minimum age in your jurisdiction).

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where the change is significant, notify you in the app. We will never change the promises in section 4 or 5 retroactively against you.

11. Contact

Questions, export requests, or deletion requests: support@kasero.app.
